Introduction

Crafted with Purpose.
Designed for Distinction.

Founded in 2020, CELARIS London began as a retail leather brand driven by a passion for craftsmanship.

A pivot towards the growing demand from corporate clients, luxury hospitality spaces and premium businesses revealed a new direction — creating bespoke leather products tailored to elevate brand experiences.

Today, CELARIS London specialises in handcrafted leather goods customised for hotels, restaurants, corporate gifting and professional environments.

Our Craft

It’s in the Details

The most refined objects are made slowly, by hand, with an obsession for the parts no one sees. Disciplined restraint — form drawn to function, character earned through material and time.
